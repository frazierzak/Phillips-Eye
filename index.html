Brandon Phillips, MD

Brandon Phillips, M.D. is a general ophthalmologist specializing in the treatment of eye diseases and disorders.

He received his medical degree from Medical College of Wisconsin and served his internship and residency at the Walter Reed National Military Medical Center. He is certified by the American Board of Ophthalmology and is also a member of the American Academy of Ophthalmology.

Dr. Phillips was born and raised in the Fayetteville area and served in the United States Army for 12 years. He currently serves on the Board of Trustees at Fayetteville State University and is an Advisory Chair for the Department of Chemistry and Physics. Dr. Phillips has been married to his wife for 15 years and they have four children together. In his spare time he enjoys powerlifting and reading philosophy.

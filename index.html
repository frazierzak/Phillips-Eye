About Us

Dr. Phillips is a general ophthalmologist whose specialty is medical diseases of the eye.

He received his medical degree from the Medical College of Wisconsin and completed internship and residency at Walter Reed National Military Medical Center. He is certified by the American Board of Ophthalmology and is a member of the American Academy of Ophthalmology.

Dr. Phillips was born and raised in the Fayetteville area. After his medical education and 12 years of military service, he came home to take care of the town that has given him so much.

Dr. Phillips and his wife, also a Fayetteville native, have four children. He currently serves on the Board of Trustees at Fayetteville State University.

In his spare time, he enjoys coaching his son's baseball team, strength training and reading.

What You Can Expect

At Phillips Eye, you will receive exceptional one-on-one care. To us, that means a treatment plan tailored to your lifestyle, a wide range of options for healthy eyes, minimal waiting, and time to talk with Dr. Phillips about your concerns.

Services

Dr. Phillips's specialty is the medical treatment of eyes. From routine care to comprehensive management of complex eye disease, his passion is your care and comfort. In addition to a wealth of experience, Dr. Phillips offers state-of-the-art diagnostic and treatment equipment, including cutting-edge intense pulsed light therapy (IPL).

Most commonly, patients visit Dr. Phillips for management of:

  • Dry eye syndrome
  • Glaucoma
  • Ocular surface disease including blepharitis and Meibomian gland dysfunction (MGD)
  • Complications of diabetes
  • Eyelid and skin conditions such as rosacea
  • Macular degeneration

Location

Phillips Eye is conveniently located in the heart of Fayetteville's healthcare district at Walter Reed Road, adjacent to the campus of Cape Fear Valley Medical Center.

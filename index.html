About Us

Dr. Phillips is a general ophthalmologist whose specialty is medical diseases of the eye.

He received his medical degree from the Medical College of Wisconsin and completed internship and residency at Walter Reed National Military Medical Center. He is certified by the American Board of Ophthalmology and is a member of the American Academy of Ophthalmology.

Dr. Phillips was born and raised in the Fayetteville area. After his medical education and 12 years of military service, he came home to take care of the town that has given him so much.

Dr. Phillips and his wife, also a Fayetteville native, have four children. He currently serves on the Board of Trustees at Fayetteville State University.

In his spare time, he enjoys coaching his son's baseball team, strength training and reading.

Our Services

Dr. Phillips’s specialty is the medical treatment of eyes. From routine care to comprehensive management of complex eye disease, his passion is your care and comfort. At Phillips Eye, you will receive exceptional, one-on-one care. To us, that means tailoring a treatment plan to your lifestyle, offering a wide range of options for healthy eyes, and a short wait, translating to time to talk with Dr. Phillips about your concerns.

Most commonly, patients visit Dr. Phillips for dry eye syndrome, ocular surface disease including blepharitis, eyelid conditions, glaucoma, meibomian gland dysfunction (MGD), complications of diabetes and macular degeneration. In addition to a wealth of experience, Dr. Phillips offers state-of-the-art diagnostic and treatment equipment, including cutting-edge intense pulsed light therapy (IPL).

Dr. Phillips’s clinic is conveniently located directly adjacent to the campus of Cape Fear Valley Medical Center on Walter Reed Road, in the heart of Fayetteville’s healthcare district.
